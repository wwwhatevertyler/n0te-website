## Objection Handling Direction

“Why not just open Obsidian?”

- Obsidian is excellent once the thought is ready for structure.
- N0te exists for the moment before that.
- The value is not another place to organize notes.
- The value is protecting flow.

“Why not Apple Notes or Stickies?”

- Those are general capture tools.
- N0te is specifically shaped for users who want rough notes to become clean Markdown in Obsidian.

“Is this too small to matter?”

- It is small by design.
- Small utilities justify themselves when they remove a repeated annoyance and feel premium enough to trust every day.
